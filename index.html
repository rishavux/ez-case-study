Brow & Beauty

Role Creative Director
Context Small Brand Design System
Platform Web Experience

"My ambition is simple & unreasonable in equal measure — to build the best branded small business in San Francisco."

Project Overview

Problem

EZ Brow & Beauty never developed a digital presence that served its identity. The existing website saw low traffic and minimal conversion, failing to fulfill its basic purpose. The business's service offerings were inaccessible on digital platforms.

Solution

A complete brand and UX system — color palette, typography, voice, and information architecture — developed through market research and direct observation, then translated into a fully redesigned mobile experience.

Delivery

A fully designed mobile menu and cart-building experience. The system preserves and strengthens the existing brand identity, while making clear offerings, with pricing & service information in detail.

The Starting Point

EZ Brow & Beauty is my mom's business, and it has sustained my family for more than a decade. She did it as a single mother and an immigrant from Nepal. I watched my mom go to work every single day, building this business from the ground up. She has loyal clientele who have stayed with her for years, and that loyalty alone is a profound accomplishment. The business deserves to shine — both digitally and physically. The story behind it is rich, but it's always been implicit, never told outright. Design is the medium through which that story could finally be crafted and shared with the world. That realization mattered to me. I could contribute to her business not just by helping it grow, but as an act of reverence — a tribute to what she's already built. I genuinely want to bring her success into a new light.

My Design Strategy

Royal, Regal & Passionate

The color palette was inherited. Shades of red, gold, and silver were present in our home, carried through objects my mom cherished for years. Growing up in Nepal, her sense of beauty was shaped by an idea of royalty. The design reflects that same regal sensibility.

Precise & Penetrative

The typography needed to feel sharp, penetrative, and precise — qualities that come directly from my mother's Type A personality. She's bold in her speech, precise in her craft, and brave in her actions. Cinzel and Spectral were chosen to reflect her presence.

Familiarity & Hospitality

The redesign needed to be made with existing clients in mind. We didn't want to disrupt what already worked. In practice, that meant preserving the same Vagaro booking flow they were familiar with, so returning felt like being welcomed back to something familiar.

Clarity & Conversion

Royalty gave the brand its color. Precision gave it its voice. Familiarity gave it trust. But none of it mattered if a client wasn't clear about the services that we offered. Every screen was designed to enhance clarity and guide users toward booking a service appointment.
